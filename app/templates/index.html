Douban movie finder is a movie recommender based on Douban movies database (the most popular Chinese movie platform). You can browse through movies you like and find similar movies to your favorite one.

Steps to find similar Movies to the ones you like:

  • Find a movie you like. You can go to the Douban platform and find its douban ID, or find a movie in our database by going directly to the Find Movie! page.
  • Enter the movies Douban ID, IMDB ID, or the movie's title. Only one is required.
  • Choose how you want to sort the similar movies.
  • Click Find.
  • Start!
